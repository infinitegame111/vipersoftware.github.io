Who are you?

Viper is a small tech team making the web open and
Secured for everyone!

Viper is planning to create many forms of tech for the world to use with privacy in mind and games not based around microtransactions. We don't rely off your data, Apps tend to use collected data as a source of payout. They might use things like your most recent searches to display ads or sold to another company. We don't like the way this is used and we collect no data such as searches or interactions to display ads or sell to another company.

Why trust us?

We strongly believe the tracking of user data is NOT ok and should not be collected from users. We will never collect user data such as recent searches, recommended, location, audio, interaction and recommended.

Who created Viper?

Viper was created by three students named Ryan, Micheal and Chase. We wanted to make a secured and fast web for everyone! We have been developing games and products such as Undone, A privacy based browser with minimal background tasks and no big watermarks or ads in browsers like edge or chrome. Socave, a fast and secure chatting app for developers or small teams. Code Spark, a simple and fast code editor allowing for fast running in python and html/css/js. These are just some of the products we plan to bring to the web in the upcoming years and we can't wait for you to try it!
